How to make tea

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Water
  • Milk -Diary/Plant-based (Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Fill up the kettle with water and boil the kettle
  2. Place a teabag in your favourite mug. Pour boiling water into your favourite mug
  3. Brew the tea for a few moments. Remove and dispose of the teabag
  4. Add milk. Add sugar
  5. Stir the tea. Enjoy the hot beverage
